PAINS

Characteristics, problems and unwanted results

• Functional
- Misdiagnosis of anemia in your child, which could cause your child to receive inappropriate treatment that would lead to side effects.
• Social
- Carrying the social stigma of not caring enough for the health of your child, since the latter suffers from anemia.
• Secondary
- Work in a health center that does not have the minimum requirements necessary to work.
- Work center far from your home.
• Unwanted sensations
- Abandonment of treatment.

Gains Creators

• With a better diet the infants will be able to develop as any infant of their age improving their academic performance.
• Provide accessibility to the early diagnosis test to people with limited resources.
• Eliminate the social stigma generated by the disease
• With the reduction of anemia in the country, it is estimated that many other diseases and discomforts will decrease in relation to it (fatigue, dizziness, headaches, tachycardia, etc.); this can be achieved in conjunction with rural development since these are the areas most affected.

FIT


Lace problem - solution
Focusing on the joys of the client, they have different frustrations and joys. In addition, they want multiple products and services. However, it is necessary to follow the path leading to the most outstanding joy which is remarkably the correct diagnosis of anemia and the type of it.

Lace product - market
Since the proposal sounds attractive and benefits both parties, both as a patient and as a doctor, we expect better advice and attention from the doctors when acting.

Lace business model
Thanks to the correct work of the health team it is estimated that the prevalence of anemia would decrease; this would lead to less loss in the GDP of the State and consequently increases in the salary of the health team to motivate them to continue like this and even improve.
